Puxun Tu


I am a fourth-year PhD student at the Institute of Biomedical Manufacturing and Life Quality Engineering, School of Mechanical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China, under the supervision of Prof. Xiaojun Chen. I had worked as a visiting Ph.D. student at the Pattern Recognition and Image Analysis (PRIA) Lab at the University of Münster in Germany, led by Prof. Xiaoyi Jiang in 2023. My research focuses on image guided surgery and augmented reality. I am the Principal Investigator of a PhD research project funded by the Natural Science Foundation of China (首批国家自然科学基金博士生项目) in 2024.

Main collaborators: Prof. Xiaojun Chen (Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China); Prof. Xiaoyi Jiang (University of Münster, Germany); Prof. Leo Joskowicz (The Hebrew University of Jerusalem, Israel); Prof. Jocelyne Troccaz (CNRS, France); Prof. Jan Egger (University Hospital Essen, Germany); Dr. Jiangchang Xu (Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China); Yao Gao (UZ Leuven, Belgium); Bolun Zeng (Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China); Yizhou Chen (University of Bern, Switzerland);
