Puxun Tu

I am a fourth-year PhD student at the Institute of Biomedical Manufacturing and Life Quality Engineering, School of Mechanical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China, under the supervision of Prof. Xiaojun Chen. I worked as a visiting Ph.D. student at the Pattern Recognition and Image Analysis (PRIA) Lab at the University of Münster in Germany, led by Prof. Xiaoyi Jiang in 2023. My research focuses on image guided surgery and augmented reality.
